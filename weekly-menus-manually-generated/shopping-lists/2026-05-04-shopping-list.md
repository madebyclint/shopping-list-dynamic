# Shopping List — May 4–9, 2026 — ~$215–$235

> **Notes:** Banana leaves are sold frozen at most Asian and Caribbean groceries in Brooklyn — try Sunrise Mart, H Mart, or any West Indian market. Buy a whole frozen pack; extras keep in the freezer for months. Makrut lime leaves and lemongrass are at Asian markets — if unavailable, dried makrut lime leaves work in a pinch. Sambal oelek is at most Asian groceries or the international aisle. Nori sheets and imitation crab are at any Asian grocery. Maggi seasoning sauce is at most bodegas and Latin/Asian markets. Gochugaru can be found at H Mart or substituted with regular red pepper flakes. **Homemade mayo:** use 2 eggs (from your weekly dozen), 1 tbsp lemon juice (from the lemon on this list), 1 tsp mustard, and ½ cup avocado oil — blend with an immersion blender. Marinate ribs the morning of or the night before for best flavor.
